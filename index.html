What is it?

WatchOut!

WatchOut! is an app that will send a notification to a pedestrian that is walking 100 meters or less near a Tram. The app will work by the mobile looking for a Bluetooth signal with the name of a registered Tram so the app knows it's a tram. Bluetooth transmitters would be placed on the fronts of a Tram sending out a non-connectable connection for so mobiles can read/recognise it.
